MY EXPERIENCE

There's a vintage which comes with age and experience.--Jon Bon Jovi

In the early 90's, my wife and I had recently designed and built a new home, had adopted our baby girl, found out we were pregnant with our 1st born son, and I had quit my job to start my own company, Total Balance, Inc. Those were exciting and scary times! As a new father and a new business owner, the most important thing for me was to find a balance between family life and running a business. Total Balance, as it turned out, was not only a perfect name for my business of testing and balancing HVAC systems but also became my work ethic. It served me well as I worked hard to balance family, business, and good health. Over the course of 26 years, I was able to grow my business into a multi-million dollar S-Corporation conducting business in a 5-state region, take the time to take care of myself, and be fully present for my family. Running a business taught me the value of hiring and training good people and to appreciate them, how to cooperate and work with HVAC design engineers and mechanical contractors to troubleshoot and solve problems, and how to handle day to day operations. My family kept me grounded and taught me that success in life is not simply the bottom line of your financial statement. As they say, it's not about the destination as much as it is about the journey! Running my own company, and raising a family has been a blessed journey. Now, the kids are grown, my wife and I are looking forward to another 30 years, and I'm ready to embark on something new. I am currently enrolled in Eleven Fifty Academy and learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I'm focused, determined,and excited to see where computer coding takes me.
